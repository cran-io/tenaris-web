Filming with a smartphone

Some cellphones are capable of filming HD videos, and there are applications that allow you to edit the video on the go or share news through live streaming.

Recommendations

Avoid sudden camera movements.
路
Move the camera using your whole body, not only your hands.
路
Decide if the video will be recorded vertically or horizontally beforehand.
路
Record dialogue only if the subject is close to the camera and the environment allows for a clear audio capture, or use an adapter to connect a lapel microphone.
