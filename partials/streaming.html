Live Streaming

Some events that are relevant for employees globally can be streamed live through certain applications. Is this the kind of video you will make?

YES
or
NO

Recommendations

Avoid sudden camera movements.
·
Move the camera using your whole body, not only your hands.
·
Decide beforehand if the video will be recorded vertically or horizontally.
·
Record dialogue only if the subject is close to the camera and the environment allows for a clear capture, or use an adapter to connect a lapel microphone.
