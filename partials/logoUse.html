Logo Use / General Specifications

The Tenaris logo is made up of two elements, the Multibar symbol and the Tenaris wordmark.

The logo is a unique piece of artwork and the proportion and arrangement of the Multibar and wordmark have been specifically determined.

It should never be typeset, recreated or altered. Any change will cause inconsistencies, ultimately diluting the impact of the brand's power. To maintain consistency, use only approved digital art files. The preferred configuration of the logo is shown here; use it whenever possible.

In all color versions of the signature, the wordmark appears in Tenaris Gray, and the Multibar is made up of Tenaris Green, Fuchsia and Blue.

Logo Use / Multibar

Correct use

When using the Tenaris logo within social media applications, it should act as the “face” of Tenaris. This is accomplished by placing the logo within the profile image area placing it on our blue, green, purple or white background.

Incorrect use
  • » Do not place the logo as part of a background image where it may be cropped by overlying content.
  • » Do not unnecessarily duplicate the presentation of the logo.
  • » Do not distort the logo.
